Cuppa

Cuppa is a mathematical scenario-modelling tool built for UK residents to run hypothetical financial simulations and explore “What If” scenarios:

  • Tax & Allowance Modelling: Use UK tax thresholds and allowances based on the inflation assumptions you provide.
  • Transparency: Illustrate long-term financial projections with a detailed year-by-year cash flow breakdown.
  • Exploration: Compare and experiment with different hypothetical outcomes by adding life events and selecting from predefined drawdown models.
  • Privacy: Built with a "Local-First" design. Your data is stored and processed on your device and not shared with us.

Important Information: Cuppa is a mathematical tool for illustrative and educational purposes only. It provides generic financial modelling, not regulated financial advice or personal recommendations. Simulations are based on user inputs and assumptions and do not account for your full personal circumstances, future legislative changes, market performance, or unmodelled income sources.

For specific financial recommendations or to discuss your personal circumstances, please consult a regulated financial professional or visit MoneyHelper (the UK’s free official guidance service).
