Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9 March 2026

Scale Realm Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to absolute data privacy. This policy applies to our website (scalerealm.co.uk) and our software products, including the Cuppa mobile application.

1. The Cuppa Application (Local-First)

Cuppa is built on a strict "Local-First" architecture.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, transmit, store, or share your personal data, financial inputs, or simulation outputs.
  • On-Device Processing: All calculations and retirement projections occur entirely on your local device hardware.
  • No Telemetry: The app contains no third-party behavioral analytics or tracking SDKs. Crash reporting is completely opt-in and must be manually sent by the user via email.

2. Website & Corporate Data

  • Website Tracking: Our website is a static page. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or visitor analytics.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us via email for support, we use your email address and any provided diagnostic data solely to resolve your issue. We do not add you to marketing lists or share your contact information with third parties.

3. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests regarding your email communications, please contact us at: info@scalerealm.co.uk.
